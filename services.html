Our Services

Reliable Excavation, Demolition & Earthmoving Solutions in the Peel Region

Single-Story Demolition

Safe removal of houses and small buildings, leaving the site clean, level, and ready for the next stage. Work is done efficiently with strong attention to safety.

Tree Removal

Tree removal using purpose-built tree grabs for safer handling, accurate placement, and efficient stacking. Ideal for block clearing, property development, and access preparation.

Rock Hammering

Breaking hard and stubborn rock using powerful rock hammer attachments, making ground suitable for construction, drainage, and foundations.

House Pads & Site Preparation

Accurate levelling and preparation for residential and rural builds. We also specialise in rock pitching to stabilise batters and slopes, and construct limestone & gravel driveways.

Excavation & Earthmoving

General excavation and bulk earthworks using 20 and 12 tonne excavators, loader, and semi tipper. Reliable, well-planned work with consistent results.

Service Area

We primarily serve the Peel Region and surrounding areas, bringing local knowledge and experience to every project. Whether it’s a small residential excavation, a landscaping project, or a large-scale commercial development, our team approaches every job with the same dedication, ensuring smooth operations and exceptional results.

Welcome to Waters Earthmoving

Waters Earthmoving is a trusted, owner-operated earthmoving business based in the Peel Region, with over 25 years of hands-on experience working across Peel and surrounding areas. We specialise in rock pitching and also carry out residential and rural earthworks, including house and shed pads, land and block clearing, tree removal, rock excavation and rock hammer work, drainage and water runoff works, and limestone and gravel driveways. All work is personally handled from start to finish, ensuring clear communication, reliable service, and quality results on every job. Our goal is simple — to deliver earthmoving work done properly, safely, and built to last.

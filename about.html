About Us

Who We Are

Waters Earthmoving is a trusted, owner-operated earthmoving business based in the Peel Region, with over 25 years of hands-on experience working across Peel and surrounding areas.

We specialise in rock pitching and also carry out residential and rural earthworks, including house and shed pads, land and block clearing, tree removal, rock excavation and rock hammer work, drainage and water runoff works, and limestone and gravel driveways. Our machinery is well maintained and fitted with the right attachments, including tree grabs for safe tree handling and rock hammers for breaking hard ground.

Whether it’s preparing a building site, improving water runoff, building limestone or gravel driveways, or stabilising slopes with rock pitching, we put the same care and attention into every job.

Because we are owner-operated, you’re not just hiring machinery — you’re dealing directly with the person doing the work. From first site visit to final job completion, the goal is simple: reliable earthmoving done properly, with results you can trust.

Our Services

Single-Story Demolition

Safe and controlled removal of homes and small buildings, ensuring the site is left clean, level, and ready for the next stage of development. We manage the process efficiently with a strong focus on safety and minimal disruption.

Tree Removal

Professional clearing using our well-designed tree grabs for safer handling, precise placement, and efficient stacking. Ideal for property development, access preparation, and block clearing.

Rock Hammering

Breaking through hard and stubborn rock using powerful rock hammer attachments, making difficult ground suitable for construction, drainage, and foundations.

House Pads & Site Preparation

Accurate levelling and preparation of land for residential and rural builds. We also specialise in rock pitching to stabilise batters and slopes, and construct limestone & gravel driveways.

Excavation & Earthmoving

General excavation and earthworks using our 20-tonne excavator, loader, and semi-tipper. From small jobs to larger site works, we deliver reliable, well-planned results every time.
